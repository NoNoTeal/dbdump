Reproduction Steps
  • Tap on invite members
  • Change the settings for all three values; i.e. Max users, 'invite expires after' and temporary membership
  • Back out of the tab without tapping on 'create link'
  • Toggle the setting to make invite link permanent (set to never expire)
  • Change invite link settings again to any value other than what you had put previously
  • Wait for 15 \~ 20 seconds and Observe
Expected Behavior

As you haven't yet created the link and are only observing the settings, it must stay on what you have applied

Current Behavior

It reverts back to whatever value you set before, when you did not create the invite
