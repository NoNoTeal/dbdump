Reproduction Steps
  • Create a server
  • Create 10 channels and attempt to sort them. You'll notice the placement shown when hovering is slightly off
  • Create 10 more channels and attempt to sort them. You'll notice the placement shown when hovering is even more off than before
  • You may repeat this process to find the problem worsens the more you do
Expected Behavior

The placement of the new channel would match where you are hovering.

Current Behavior

The more channels and further down the channel list you go, the more inaccurate and the larger the offset is when sorting channels.
